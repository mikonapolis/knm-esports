About KnM

KnM, also known as Kupunan ni Miko, is a Dubai-based Dota 2 esports team formed by seven close friends who share a passion for competitive gaming. Built on teamwork, trust, and chemistry, the team continues to grow together through every match and challenge they face. More than just a team, KnM represents friendship, dedication, and the drive to compete at a higher level.
